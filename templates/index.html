AI Trading Intelligence

ChaseInGreen

Beta testing

ChaseInGreen combines market analysis, risk controls, execution planning, journaling and personal performance learning in one trading environment. It helps traders understand current conditions, decide whether to enter or wait, calculate position size and review how their own decisions affected the outcome.

  • Market-state, pressure and trend interpretation
  • Support, resistance and execution-level planning
  • Position sizing, risk awareness and profit protection
  • Trade journaling, outcomes and calendar analytics
  • Personal learning from discipline and past behavior

Lottery Analytics

Lottovate

Beta returning

Lottovate is a data-assisted Daily 3 and Daily 4 platform built around historical results, user-selected numbers, pattern analysis and platform-generated predictions. It is designed to make lottery data easier to explore without claiming that any result is guaranteed.

  • Daily 3 and Daily 4 prediction tools
  • Midday and evening draw analysis
  • Hot, cold and historical number intelligence
  • Personal picks, easy picks and platform predictions
  • Confidence tracking and prediction performance history
